# Creative Director Review Gate

Optional review layer after audit, before any rewrite or rebuild.

## What it checks

- Is the diagnosis complete, or does it raise more questions than it answers?
- Does the priority order match the actual business impact?
- Is the audit missing anything obvious (target audience, competitive context, brand constraints)?
- Would acting on this diagnosis actually move the needle?

## Verdicts

- **APPROVE** — diagnosis is ready, proceed to rewrite/rebuild
- **REVISE** — diagnosis needs more work before proceeding
- **REJECT** — wrong layer entirely, restart from intake

## When to use

Use when the audit will lead to a paid rewrite or rebuild service. Skip for free quick scans.
